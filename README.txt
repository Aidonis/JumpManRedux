Retro Donkey-Kong Look-A-Like
By Aidan Nabass

This is a prototype of a Donkey Kong like platformer staring JumpMan Mk1.
The player traverses the platforms and ladders to make it to the top platform
while avoiding the rolling boulders.
Score points by spending time above the boulders!

Controls:

Movement:
A - Left
D - Right
W - Climb Up
S - Climb Down
Space - Jump
